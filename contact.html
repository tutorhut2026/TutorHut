Submit an application via our Tutor Registration page. Our admin team reviews your qualifications and details. Once approved, your profile is published and students can request sessions with you.

Browse tutors freely without an account. When you're ready to request a session, register as a student, then submit a tuition request for your chosen tutor, subject, day, and time. Our admin team will approve and share the tutor's contact details with you.

To protect both tutors and students, contact details are only shared once a tuition request has been reviewed and approved by our admin team. This ensures every connection on TutorHut is safe, verified, and intentional.

Tutor applications are typically reviewed within 3–5 working days. Tuition requests are reviewed within 1–2 working days. You'll receive an email notification either way.
