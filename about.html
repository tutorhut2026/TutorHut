Our story, mission, and the people who make it happen.

Our mission

Bridging the Gap Between Students and Expert Tutors

TutorHut was founded with a clear purpose: to make quality tuition accessible to every student, while providing a trusted platform for expert tutors to share their knowledge.

We believe that every student deserves access to a qualified tutor who can deliver focused, personalised instruction. At the same time, we know that tutors need a reliable, professional platform to connect with committed learners.

That's why we built TutorHut — a carefully moderated marketplace where tutors are individually vetted and approved by our admin team before they can take on students.
